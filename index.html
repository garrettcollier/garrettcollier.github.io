Who am I?

A Software Developer from Arkansas

Garrett Collier holds a Master of Public Administration (MPA) from the University of Southern California. He values family, his faith in our Lord and Savior, and dedicates his work to improving the lives of others through both technology and public service. Garrett has a background in Computer Science at Hendrix College, where he focused on software development, database design, process automation, accounting, and economics. He further expanded his academic experience through summer research at Michigan State University, before applying his skills professionally as a Solution Developer at Acxiom LLC.

In addition to his technical career, Garrett is actively pursuing his long-term goals in local government, politics, and nonprofit leadership. He serves as a software developer for The "I Love U Guys" Foundation, where he leads website development and digital infrastructure to support school safety protocols across the country. This role allows him to bridge public administration with technology, collaborating with educators, first responders, and administrators to strengthen crisis response and reunification methods. Garrett is committed to making an impact in both the technical and public service arenas, laying the foundation for future leadership in his community and state.
